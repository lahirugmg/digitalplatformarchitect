# IMP-018: Visual-first teaching patterns

**Status:** Proposed
**Priority:** High
**Category:** Learning Experience
**Effort:** Medium
**Created:** 2026-02-15

## Description

Adopt visual-first pedagogy with high-fidelity diagrams, focusing on progressive reveal and clarity of system interactions.

## Proposed Enhancements

- Use progressive reveal animations for complex diagrams
- Add labeled stages for each reveal step
- Provide short callouts to explain each layer

## Benefits

- Faster comprehension of complex systems
- Better retention through visual sequencing
- Reduced cognitive overload

## Acceptance Criteria

- Each complex diagram has at least 3 progressive steps
- Users can replay or scrub steps
- All steps include short text explanations

## Estimated Effort

- Medium (2-3 weeks)
