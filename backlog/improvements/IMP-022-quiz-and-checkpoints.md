# IMP-022: Quiz and knowledge checkpoints

**Status:** Proposed
**Priority:** Medium
**Category:** Learning Validation
**Effort:** Small
**Created:** 2026-02-15

## Description

Add short quizzes and decision checkpoints after modules to test understanding and trade-offs.

## Proposed Enhancements

- Multiple-choice questions tied to modules
- Instant feedback and explanations
- Track completion state per module

## Benefits

- Reinforces learning
- Helps validate understanding of trade-offs

## Acceptance Criteria

- Each major module has at least 1 quiz
- Feedback is immediate and explanatory
- Progress is stored locally

## Estimated Effort

- Small (1-2 weeks)
